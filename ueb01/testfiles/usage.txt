Usage:

  ueb01 -h

  shows this help and exits.

  - or -

  ueb01 NUM OP

  checks whether a given number is prime or a happy number or finds the
  divisors of a given number. A call always consists of a number followed
  by an operation, where:

  NUM has to be a positive integer number (including 0).

  OP is one of:
    p checks whether NUM is prime or not
    d looks for the dividers of NUM
    h checks whether NUM is a happy number
